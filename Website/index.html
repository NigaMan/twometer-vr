TwometerVR

TwometerVR is a FREE open-source alternative to commercial hand trackers such as Oculus Touch or PS Move.

I started building this because I wanted to play Beat Saber without having to buy a new VR headset, but it got a little out of hand, so here we go building a full Virtual Reality controller system...

Learn more
Note: It's working, but still under development. That means fancy stuff like setup wizards may not be 100% complete and you may encounter bugs

Features

  • Very low cost, less than $20 for two controllers
  • Works with almost every consumer-grade webcam
  • Compatible with SteamVR (yes, the Beat Saber part)
  • Fully open-source and free
  • Nice DIY project

Getting Started

So, how do I get these controllers, you may ask. Well, I am currently not in the position to mass-produce these things or even build kits for them, so for now, there are build instructions, which you can find here.

I am also planning to make a build video on these which will be linked here, but that will take a while probably...

After it's done and working, download the software, set it up with the wizard and start playing!
